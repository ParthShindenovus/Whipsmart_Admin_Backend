===Q&A-002===
Q: How does WhipSmart help employees with vehicle ownership?  
A: WhipSmart provides employees with a way to buy their dream car and pay for its running costs (fuel, insurance, tyres, servicing, registration, etc.) through a single monthly fee via salary sacrifice. Employees can pay with pre-tax earnings and purchase their vehicle GST-free.
